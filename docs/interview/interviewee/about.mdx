---

The length of these interviews is usually between 45 min and 1.5 hrs,
each interview is usually divided into three sections: 

1. [Self introductions](#introductions)
2. [Technical exercise](#tech-exercise)
3. [Ask anything about the company](#ask-anything)

Note: Some interviews may also include [behavioral questions](#behavioral)
and/or [specific technical Q&A](#tech-qa), and sometimes they might even replace technical exercise
with these sections.
